Whirlpool Appliance Repair

Founded in 1911 and headquartered in Michigan, Whirlpool has been an American’s golden child appliance manufacturer. Throughout the years, Whirlpool has been a company that we generally recommend for dishwashers, fridges, dryers, and ovens. They are also the parent company to Maytag, Jennair, and Kitchen-Aid.

Common Issues

🢒 Whirlpool, like most appliance manufacturers, has had their fair share of recalls and issues.

Cabrio actuator styler washer

🢒 We run into these often. For whatever reason, Whirlpool has moved away from their proven engineering on the prior direct model style washers. General life spans of these specific washers can be exceptionally short.

Best appliances they make

🢒 Dryers, dishwashers, oven, fridges, and a few select styles of washers.

GE Appliance Repair

GE is a multi-nation, multi-division company based out of Boston. GE’s appliance division has been hit or miss over the years and sometimes seems like an afterthought when it comes to greater company products. GE engineering on their appliances is notorious for changing up the game and updating appliances with new features and better looks, sometimes at the determinant of quality. The cooking lines are generally above average over the years.

Common Issues

🢒 GE has had multiple issues with any appliances that use water. Dishwashers, fridges, and washers usually fight us tooth and nail to diagnose. This doesn’t necessarily mean they are bad products, but rather lead to longer diagnosis times.

🢒 GE is often said as “generally expensive” around the office because their parts are more expensive than other brands.

🢒 French door style fridges almost always develop a fridge fan issue. This is usually resolved by an update and a new fan motor.

🢒 Ge Dishwashers are engineered with poor cleaning performance, which results in unsatisfied customers.

🢒 Like the dishwashers, GE washers often have a drain, control board, and motor issues. These lead to very costly repairs early on in their life span.

Best appliances they make

🢒 Microwaves and Ovens.

Samsung Appliance Repair

What is there to say about the South Korean superpower that is Samsung. They make everything from your phone to your fridge. They have expanded their market share by joining the appliance game only recently.

Common Issues

🢒 Due to poor quality control issues in manufacturing, their compressors and sealed systems in their fridges are not great. The average life span on a refrigerator before it needs considerable repair is only a couple of years.

🢒 Some bearing issues with front load washers happen, but they aren’t any worse than anyone else in the market. This issue seems to be just avoidable at this point, no matter what brand you select.

🢒 Samsung makes beautiful appliances with loads of features, and with that comes multiple fail points. Often they over-promise with a long list of features at the expense of quality.

Best appliances they make

🢒 Washers, Refrigerator and Dryers.

LG Appliance Repair
